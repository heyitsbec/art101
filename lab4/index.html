Challenge

For this lab we had to pseudocode an everyday task that most students may not be familar with and also a computer task. We chose to write about checking the oil in a car and a minesweeper game. Then we had to turn these steps into Javascirpt comments and add them into our index.file. Finally we had to create this page!

Problems
